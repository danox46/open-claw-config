## How a Hobby Gets Here

A hobby is added to this list (and to `hobbies.global.hobbies_list` in MongoDB) only after it passes the pitch conversion criteria:
- **Consistency** — attended/practiced at the target frequency for 2+ weeks
- **Commitment** — enrolled, purchased equipment, or otherwise committed
- **Time** — sessions fit comfortably into the weekly schedule

Use the `hobby-pitch` skill to run a conversion. That skill inserts into `hobbies.global.hobbies_list`, creates the full hobby folder/collections, and updates this file.
