# Research Summary: LinkedIn Smoke Test — AI Consulting Lead Gen

## 1. Campaign — Audience Pain Points & Competitive Positioning

**Primary Audience:** Mid-market operations leaders (COOs, VPs of Operations, Directors of Digital Transformation) trying to operationalize AI adoption.

**Core Pain Points (Validated):**
- **Scaling Beyond Pilots:** 80% of AI initiatives stall at pilot stage; only 20% scale to production
- **Data Quality:** Poor data affects 45% of AI projects; siloed systems hinder integration
- **Integration Barriers:** 73% cite legacy infrastructure as primary roadblock
- **Talent Gap:** 42-43% lack in-house AI expertise
- **ROI Uncertainty:** Only 25% of AI initiatives deliver expected returns; "AI proof gap" is critical

**Competitive Landscape:**
- **Big Players:** Accenture, McKinsey, IBM Consulting dominate enterprise space
- **Specialized Firms:** LeewayHertz, EffectiveSoft, Intellectyx AI focus on niche AI workflows
- **Mid-Market Gap:** Enterprise solutions too complex; SMB solutions lack scale → **Opportunity:** "Enterprise capabilities without enterprise complexity"

**Key Messages That Land:**
- "Move from Pilot to Production: Cut Time-to-Value by 40%"
- "AI Audits That Reveal Hidden ROI (Not Just Risks)"
- "Integration-First AI: Connect to Your Existing Stack, Not Replace It"
- "Proven ROI Within 90 Days or We Pay the Difference"

## 4. Forms — Capture Strategy & Gates

| Funnel Stage | Fields | Conversion Impact |

| Mid-funnel | 5-7 (add job title, revenue range) | 0.81% conversion |

- 5 minutes = 21X conversion

- **Integration:** LinkedIn Lead Gen Forms → CRM automation within 5 minutes

## 6. Lead Rules — Scoring Signals & Thresholds

**3-Block Lead Scoring Framework:**

**Block 1 — FIT (0-50 points):**
- Company matches ICP (size + sector + zone): +10
- Decision-maker or influencer: +15
- No negative signals (competitor, partner, blacklist): +5
- Size $10M–$100M: +10
- Job title VP/Director: +10

**Block 2 — INTENT (0-50 points):**
- Pricing/demo page visit: +15
- ROI calculator download: +20
- Webinar attendance: +10
- Case study download: +10
- Multiple page views (+5/session): +5/session

**Block 3 — TIMING (0-40 points):**
- Active project with deadline: +20
- Timeline <30 days: +25
- Recent trigger event (hiring, fundraise, migration): +15
- Budget allocated: +20

**Thresholds:**
- **0-39:** Nurture (bottom of funnel)
- **40-59:** MQL (48h outreach)
- **60-79:** SQL (24h outreach)
- **80+:** Hot lead (1h outreach)

**Positive Signals (+10 to +30):**
- Visited pricing/demo page: +15
- Downloaded ROI calculator: +20
- Attended webinar: +10
- Requested case study: +10
- Job title: Ops Manager/Director: +15
- Timeline <30 days: +25

**Negative Signals (-5 to -10):**
- Visited only homepage: -3
- Downloaded blog post only: -5
- Job title: Individual contributor: -10
- No engagement in 14+ days: -5

## 7. Sales Handoff — Qualification & Data Requirements

**Qualification Criteria (BANT-Q):**
- **Budget:** $50K+ confirmed
- **Authority:** Decision-maker or influence
- **Need:** Clear pain identified (manual processes, cost issues)
- **Timeline:** 30-90 days to decision
- **Quantity:** Locations, warehouse size, volume
- **Current Solution:** Vendor name, pain points

**Data Points Sales Needs:**
1. Contact info (name, email, phone, authority)
2. Company context (size, revenue, locations, pains)
3. Engagement history (content downloaded, pages viewed)
4. Pain point priorities (top 3 challenges)
5. Buying committee (IT, Finance, Operations roles)
6. Competitive context (current vendor, blockers)
7. Timeline (decision + implementation dates)
8. Budget (range, approval status, payment terms)

**AI-Enhanced Handoff:**
- AI chat qualifiers achieve 28-40% conversion (vs. 2-3% for traditional forms)
- AI-qualified leads convert 3.2x higher than unqualified form submissions
- Signal-based AI prospecting achieves 5-25% reply rates (vs. ~3% traditional)
