# Strategist Feedback - PulseHR Mid-Market Momentum

## Alignment Issues Identified

1. **Missing Core Blog Post**: Need "7 Signs Your HR Tech Stack is Holding Your Mid-Market Company Back" to identify pain points early in the funnel
2. **Compliance Content Gap**: "How to Automate Compliance Without Breaking the Bank" blog post missing - critical for mid-market HR managers concerned about labor law complexity
3. **Case Study Repetition**: Only TechStart case study in plan - need additional social proof from different industry/mid-market
4. **Whitepaper Title Misalignment**: "Scaling HR Technology" should be "The Mid-Market HR Tech Playbook: Scaling Without Chaos" to match campaign positioning
5. **Webinar Topic Mismatch**: "AI in HR" not aligned with core campaign messages - should focus on "Mastering Workforce Management: From 50 to 500 Employees" instead
6. **Landing Pages Missing**: Compliance automation and webinar registration landing pages not specified
7. **Forms Missing**: Compliance inquiry and webinar registration forms not defined
8. **Email Sequences Missing**: Compliance awareness, hiring automation, and playbook nurture sequences not in plan
9. **Lead Rules Missing**: Scoring criteria and routing rules not documented
10. **Sales Handoff Missing**: SQA threshold and handoff process not defined

## Recommended Revisions

Revise all 16 assets to address above gaps. Add 4-5 new assets for compliance content, webinar content, and lead management. Remove AI webinar and replace with workforce management webinar. Ensure all assets align with core campaign positioning: "HR Tech built specifically for mid-market HR teams."
