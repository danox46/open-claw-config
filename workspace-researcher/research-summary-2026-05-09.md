## 1. Campaign Strategy

### Audience Pain Points (Validated)

| Priority | Pain Point | Evidence |
|----------|------------|----------|
| **H1** | **Scaling Beyond Pilot Projects** — 78% of mid-market companies have adopted AI, but only 20% successfully scale beyond pilots | Source: SearchLab 2026 |
| **H1** | **Integration with Legacy Systems** — 73% cite integration as primary roadblock | Source: SoftWebSolutions |
| **H1** | **Unclear ROI** — Up to 95% of AI projects fail to deliver measurable ROI; 25% deliver expected returns | Source: Terminal-X AI |
| **H2** | **Data Quality & Availability** — Poor, fragmented data undermines AI initiatives | Source: MesComputing |
| **H2** | **Skills Shortages** — Lack of in-house AI talent forces reliance on external expertise | Source: Isometrik AI |
| **H3** | **High Implementation Costs** — Budget constraints limit adoption | Source: MaiaBrain |

### Competitive Positioning Opportunities

**Market Gaps:**
1. **Mid-Market Specialization** — Most competitors focus on enterprise (Big 4 firms charge $300–600+/hr vs. boutique $150–300/hr). Opportunity: "Enterprise capabilities without enterprise complexity."
2. **Build vs. Advise** — Top firms (RTS Labs, LeewayHertz, TFSF Ventures) emphasize implementation, not just strategy. Opportunity: "We deploy, not just advise."
3. **Agentic AI Focus** — Autonomous AI agents are emerging trend. Opportunity: Lead with agentic workflows.

**Positioning Statement:**
> "Fresh Run Demo bridges the gap between AI promise and business outcomes for mid-market operations companies. We don't just strategize—we implement, integrate, and prove ROI within 6–18 months."

### Key Messages That Will Land

| Audience Segment | Message | Reason |
|------------------|---------|--------|
| **Visionary CEO/Owner** | "Scale AI beyond pilots with proven ROI in 6–18 months" | Addresses primary concern about pilot failure |
| **Practical Operations Manager** | "Seamless integration with your existing systems—no workflow disruption" | Solves integration roadblock |

## 4. Forms

### Form Strategy by Funnel Stage

| Stage | Fields | Conversion Rate | Purpose |
|-------|--------|-----------------|---------|
| **Top-of-Funnel** | 2–3 (name, company, email) | 3.20% | Low-friction lead capture |
| **Mid-Funnel** | 5–7 (add job title, revenue range, employee count) | 2.10% | Qualify fit |
| **Bottom-of-Funnel** | 8–10 (add timeline, budget, current solution) | 0.81% | Sales handoff |

### Progressive Profiling Example

**Initial Contact:**
- Name
- Company
- Email
- CTA: "Get Your Free Implementation Plan"

**Second Touch (after email open):**
- Job Title
- Current AI Tools
- CTA: "Schedule Technical Assessment"

**Third Touch (after demo request):**
- Revenue Range
- Employee Count
- Implementation Timeline
- Budget Status
- Current Vendor (if any)
- CTA: "Book Consultation"

### Form Friction Insights
- **1-field forms:** 3.20% conversion
- **5-field forms:** 0.81% conversion
- **Leads contacted within 1 hour:** Convert 7X more than those waited on
- **Leads contacted within 5 minutes:** Convert 21X more than those waited 1 hour

## 6. Lead Rules

### Lead Scoring Framework (3-Block Model)

**Block 1: FIT (Ideal Customer Profile)** — 0–50 points
- Company matches ICP (size + sector + zone): +10
- Contact is decision-maker or influencer: +15
- No negative signals (competitor, partner, blacklist): +5
- Size $10M–$100M revenue: +10
- Job title VP/Director/Manager: +10

**Block 2: INTENT (Behavioral Signals)** — 0–50 points
- Visited pricing/demo/implementation page: +15
- Downloaded ROI calculator: +20
- Attended webinar: +10
- Requested case study: +10
- Multiple page views (demo content): +5 per session

**Block 3: TIMING (Buying Window)** — 0–40 points
- Active project declared with deadline: +20
- Purchase timeline < 30 days: +25
- Recent trigger event (hiring, fundraise, migration): +15
- Budget allocated: +20

### Scoring Thresholds
| Score | Classification | Action |
|-------|----------------|--------|
| **40+** | MQL | Nurture sequence |
| **60+** | SQL | 24-hour outreach |
| **80+** | Hot Lead | 1-hour outreach |

### Positive Signals (+10 to +30 points)
- Visited pricing/demo/implementation page: +15
- Downloaded ROI calculator: +20
- Attended webinar: +10
- Requested case study: +10
- Multiple page views (demo content): +5 per session
- Job title: Ops Manager/Director: +15
- Timeline < 30 days: +25

### Negative Signals (-5 to -10 points)
- Visited only homepage: -3
- Downloaded blog post only: -5
- Job title: Individual contributor: -10
- No engagement in 14+ days: -5

## 7. Sales Handoff Requirements

### Qualification Criteria (BANT-Q)

| Criteria | Requirement |
|----------|-------------|
| **Budget** | $50K+ budget confirmed |
| **Authority** | Decision maker or has influence |
| **Need** | Clear pain identified (manual processes, cost issues) |
| **Timeline** | 30–90 days to decision |
| **Quantity** | Locations, warehouse size, volume |
| **Current Solution** | Vendor name, pain points |

### Data Points Sales Needs

1. **Contact Info:** Name, email, phone, authority level
2. **Company Context:** Size, revenue, locations, pains
3. **Engagement History:** Content downloaded, pages viewed
4. **Pain Point Priorities:** Top 3 challenges
5. **Buying Committee:** IT, Finance, Operations roles
6. **Competitive Context:** Current vendor, blockers
7. **Timeline:** Decision + implementation dates
8. **Budget:** Range, approval status, payment terms

### SLAs
- **80+ hot lead:** 1-hour response
- **60–79 SQL:** 24-hour response
- **40–59 MQL:** 48-hour response
