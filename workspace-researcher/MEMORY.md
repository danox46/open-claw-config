## Lead Generation Framework

### MQL Definition
- Minimum score: 40 points
- Required: Email, Company, Job Title
- Source: Tracked content engagement

### SQL Definition
- Minimum score: 60 points
- Required: All mid-funnel fields + phone
- Timeline: "Within 30-90 days"
- Budget status: "Already allocated" or "Approved range"

### Lead Scoring Signals (3-Block Framework)

**Block 1 - FIT (Ideal Customer Profile)**
- Company matches ICP (size + sector + zone)
- Contact is decision-maker or influencer
- No negative signals (competitor, partner, blacklist)

**Block 2 - INTENT (Behavioral Signals)**
- Demo or contact request: Strong signal → immediate action
- Pricing page visit (2+ times): Strong signal
- 2+ asset downloads: Medium signal
- Repeated email opens: Medium signal
- Webinar attendance: Medium signal

**Block 3 - TIMING (Buying Window)**
- Active project declared with deadline: Open window
- Purchase timeline < 30 days: Urgency identified
- Recent trigger event (hiring, fundraise, migration): New/accelerated need

**Positive (+10 to +30 points):**
- Visited pricing/demo page: +15
- Downloaded ROI calculator: +20
- Attended webinar: +10
- Requested case study: +10
- Multiple page views (demo content): +5 per session
- Job title: Ops Manager/Director: +15
- Timeline < 30 days: +25

**Negative (-5 to -10 points):**
- Visited only homepage: -3
- Downloaded blog post only: -5
- Job title: Individual contributor: -10
- No engagement in 14+ days: -5

### Trigger-Based Sequences
| Trigger | Duration | Email Count | Goal |
|---------|----------|-------------|------|
| Website → demo page | 10 days | 5 emails | Demo request |
| Download → checklist | 14 days | 7 emails | Education + demo |
| Webinar registration | 7 days | 5 emails | Attend + convert |
| Demo request | 3 days | 4 touches | Meeting confirmation |
| No-show demo | 10 days | 3 touches | Reschedule |

### Subject Line Patterns
- Awareness: "5 signs your logistics software is costing you money"
- Consideration: "Inventory optimization checklist (free download)"
- Decision: "ROI calculator for logistics software"

### Best Practices
- Under 150 words per email
- 1 clear CTA per email
- Customer quotes for social proof
- Send Mon-Thu, 9-11am (mid-market schedule)
- Progressive profiling: Add 1 field per interaction
- Trigger-based: Respond within 1 hour for high-intent leads
- Adaptive paths: Adjust based on engagement signals

### MQL→SQL Workflow (9 Steps)
1. FIT data collection (Marketing)
2. Automatic scoring FIT+INTENT+TIMING (Tool)
3. MQL verification (Marketing Ops)
4. Nurture if not SQL-ready (Marketing)
5. SQL validation checklist (Marketing Ops)
6. Routing to sales rep (Marketing Ops/CRM)
7. First sales contact within SLA (Sales)
8. Sales→marketing feedback (Sales)
9. Monthly improvement loop (Marketing+Sales)

### SQL-Ready Checklist (Minimum Conditions)
- FIT: Company matches ICP, decision-maker identified, no blacklist signals
- INTENT: At least 1 strong signal (demo/pricing visit/download), score ≥ threshold
- TIMING: Project/need mentioned, no recent "not now", valid contact data
